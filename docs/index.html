Glimpse Image Editor

Glimpse is an open source image editor based on the GNU Image Manipulation Program. The goal is to experiment with new ideas and expand the use of free software.

Download »

Overview

Show Off Your Imagination

Use a complete set of professional photography tools to turn your snapshots into works of art. Adjust, crop, remove objects, retouch, and repair old photos. Play with color, effects, and more to turn the ordinary into something extraordinary.

For Any Blemish

Fix common imperfections like red eye, lens distortion, and more with the powerful filters and corrective tools Glimpse provides. You can even have some fun animating your photos too!
